Your presets

On this page, you can select presets with settings for your preferred environment in your spot - either your own, or one of our pre-built presets.

Press a preset to view the settings. From here, you can select the preset, or - if it is one of your own presets - delete it.

If you wish to change- or create a new preset, you need to select a preset, make your changes and save the preset from there.
