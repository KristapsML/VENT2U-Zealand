Selecting your spot

Our goal is to make you feel good, right in your place of work. For this to happen, we need to know where this place exactly is.

If you're in the same spot as the last time you used this system, you can quickly jump back in. If not, you can quickly select your new spot.

If you generally change spots a lot, you can tell us to skip this step for you. You can always change this in your settings.
